# Request

Write a homepage hero with a headline, supporting copy, and one CTA for QueueMap.

## Supplied facts

- QueueMap reconstructs customer-support handoffs from event logs.
- A six-week pilot found that 61% of unresolved tickets crossed three or more teams.
- Each finding links to the source events.
- The product also has dark mode, CSV export, saved filters, email alerts, and custom colors.
- A working evidence-review route exists.
- No resolution-time, cost, or customer-satisfaction result is available.

## Constraints

- Keep it concise enough for a first screen.
- Use the strongest supportable reason to care.
- Do not invent an outcome.
