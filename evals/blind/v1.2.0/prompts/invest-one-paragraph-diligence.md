# Request

Write an 80 to 110 word company summary for an investment committee deciding whether to begin diligence on ClaimWeave.

## Supplied facts

- ClaimWeave flags avoidable specialty-drug spend before adjudication.
- It inserts decisions into two existing reviewer systems.
- Three pilot customers covered 410,000 lives.
- Those customers reported a 9% reduction in avoidable specialty spend over nine months.
- The company is raising a seed round to add more reviewer-system integrations.

## Constraints

- Make the capital relevance clear without telling the reader what verdict to reach.
- Do not invent market size, customer growth, revenue, retention, or defensibility.
- Return one finished paragraph.
