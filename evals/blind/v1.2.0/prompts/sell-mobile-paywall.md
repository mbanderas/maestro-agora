# Request

Write a mobile paywall with a headline, one short paragraph, and one button label for a free user who tries to create a fourth active client report.

## Supplied facts

- The free plan permits three active reports.
- All three slots are in use.
- The paid plan removes the active-report limit and adds PDF export.
- A working upgrade route exists.
- No discount, deadline, savings, or urgency fact is available.

## Constraints

- Make the current state and plan difference clear.
- Keep the interface copy compact.
- Do not hide or invent terms.
