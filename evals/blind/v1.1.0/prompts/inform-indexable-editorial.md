# Request

Write a 100 to 130 word opening for a public educational article explaining why allowing an AI crawler does not guarantee that a page will be selected or cited in an answer.

## Supplied facts

- Crawler access can affect eligibility to retrieve a page.
- Access alone does not guarantee retrieval, selection, quotation, citation, ranking, recommendation, conversion, or revenue.
- AI products can use distinct agents for search, training, or user-requested retrieval.
- Product and crawler behavior can change, so technical statements need current official support.

## Constraints

- Lead with the plain-language answer.
- Preserve the difference between access and selection.
- Do not turn the article into a product pitch or invent a source.
